Hi! Welcome to my website.

My name is Naomi and I am a 20 year old Graphic Designer, currently living in Traralgon, Victoria. I've spent the past 3 years living in Melbourne and studying at Monash University, Caulfield Campus. My specialities are branding and publication design, however I have been known to dabble in photography, videography and coding (yes, I did code this website by hand).

I have gathered some working experience through my year long stint as a Publicity Officer for Monash University DanceSport Club (now Monash Dance Society) and from interning at Stockdale & Leggo for about 4 and a half months during 2019.

Since getting my first taste of being a professional designer during my internship at Stockdale & Leggo, I have been very eager to jump into the working world!

In my spare time, I enjoy dancing (ask me and I will give you a long list of styles I have tried), playing Korfball, learning French, reading and spending time with my friends.

I'm always up for a chat, so feel free to shoot me an email whenever you like!
