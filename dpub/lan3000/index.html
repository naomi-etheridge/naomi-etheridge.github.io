About us!


Short Reads Book Club was set up with busy students who miss reading in mind.

Studying and long days at class make it hard to pick up a book for fun, let alone finish that book. Our mission is to give you some book recommendations that are less than 300 pages long that you should be able to pick up from your student library, local community library, or access online as an ebook or audio book, and encourage more students to have some more wind-down time with a book instead of Netflix.
